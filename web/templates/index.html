What makes Nestling Bot different

We're an open source competitor to MEE6. Nestling bot has games, moderation commands, a bot panel, counting setup, suggestion setup, and other fun commands that you can use.
Nestling Bot is also a solo project that BlueRobin (me) made.

Here are some commands:

  • /2048 (runs a game of 2048 IN DISCORD!)
  • /rockpaperscissors (play rock paper scissors with friends)
  • /imaging emoji-art (generates emoji art from an image within discord!)
  • And many more commands related to moderation and server management.
